Changes you make to your RADIUS servers must be reviewed by the Roaming Operator before they are pushed into production. Your changes here will be queued, and will typically be processed within one working day. You will be contacted once this has happened. If you need to co-ordinate a service-affecting change (e.g. to change a shared secret), please make the change here first and then contact us to schedule a time before making the change on your infrastructure.

Enabling RADIUS over TLS (RadSec) requires mutual certificate authentication using a certificate issued by the eduroam Certificate Service. Please ensure you have a valid certificate installed on your RADIUS server before enabling RadSec.

If you need a RadSec certificate, please add your server below with the correct External Hostname and then supply a matching certificate signing request (CSR) to the Roaming Operator.

If your RADIUS software supports it, you may wish to consider using RADIUS TLS-PSK (Pre-Shared Key) instead.
